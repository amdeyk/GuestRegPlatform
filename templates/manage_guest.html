Manage Guest Activities

How to Use This Page:
  • Guest Check-In Management: Click this button to navigate to the check-in management page. Here, you can update the status of guests as they arrive, ensuring that their attendance is accurately recorded.
  • Payment Status Tracking: Use this option to manage and confirm payment receipts. It helps in keeping track of financial transactions for each guest, making sure all payments are accounted for.
  • Gift Distribution Records: This button leads you to the gift management section. Here, you can record which guests have received their gifts, helping in efficiently managing and distributing gifts.
  • Car Service Management: Select this to update the status of car services provided to guests. Whether it's a rental or a shuttle service, this feature ensures that all transportation arrangements are tracked.
  • Meal Coupon Distribution: Head to this section to manage meal coupons. It allows you to distribute, track, and manage meal coupons for each day, ensuring all guests receive their entitled meals.
  • Simplified Guest Management: Each of these buttons is designed to streamline specific guest management tasks. By providing a centralized and organized way to handle different aspects of guest services, these tools help in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of event management.
